Welcome, I'm David.

Data Analyst - Data Visualization

Data Analyst with experience in the education and oil and gas industries. My background has taught me how to explain technical concepts, remote sensing, data acquisition, and how data impacts business decisions. Recognized as proactive, resourceful, and persistent problem-solver. Looking to leverage skill sets to provide analytics to key stakeholders to make data-driven decisions.

I earned a certificate in Data Analytics and Visualization through Rice University with skills in Python, R, VBA, and SQL. Recently completed a project in a team of three using data sets from the National Weather Service and the EPA and using Python, JavaScript, and Flask to create an app that predicts the bacteria risk levels of coastal waters. My analytical mindset, diverse professional experience, and personal interests provide a unique toolset for finding solutions and working with people.
